Nuclear Risk Scenario

Description

Nuclear technology, whether as weapon or power source, creates existential stakes that test deterrence theory, containment, and human error margins.

Real-World Relevance

Applicable to nuclear deterrence policy, reactor safety, waste management, and proliferation risk assessment.
