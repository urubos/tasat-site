Youth Resistance Movement Against Government Suppression

Description

Young people with special abilities, suppressed and imprisoned by the government, organize a resistance movement, testing whether youthful idealism can overcome institutional power.

Real-World Relevance

Models youth activist movements, relevant to climate strikes, pro-democracy student movements, and the dynamics of youth organizing against entrenched power structures.
