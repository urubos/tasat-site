Persecuted Ability Minority

Description

A minority group with innate abilities (psychic, magical, genetic) faces institutional persecution from a dominant authority (church, state), testing whether integration or separation is the viable path for communities whose capabilities threaten the power structure.

Real-World Relevance

Models real-world dynamics of minority persecution, religious institutional power, and debates about whether enhanced individuals require protection or containment.
