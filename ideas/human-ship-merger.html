Human-Machine Consciousness Merger

Description

A pilot's consciousness merges permanently with a starship's electronic systems, creating a hybrid intelligence that is neither human nor machine, testing whether merged consciousness retains personhood or becomes something new.

Real-World Relevance

Models brain-computer interface outcomes: relevant to Neuralink, consciousness upload, and the ethics of permanent human-machine integration.
