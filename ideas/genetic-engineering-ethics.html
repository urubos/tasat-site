Genetic Engineering Ethics

Description

Deliberate manipulation of biological code creates organisms or human variants that challenge existing ethical and legal frameworks.

Real-World Relevance

Informs debates on CRISPR gene editing, designer organisms, and the boundaries of human enhancement.
