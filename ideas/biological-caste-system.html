Biological Caste System

Description

A society stratifies its population by a biological trait (blood color) into rulers, workers, and slaves, with magic/power allocated by caste, testing whether biological determinism can justify permanent hierarchy.

Real-World Relevance

Models race-based caste systems, genetic determinism arguments, and any social order that treats biological traits as legitimate bases for hierarchical power distribution.
