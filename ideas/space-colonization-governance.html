Space Colonization Governance

Description

Establishing permanent settlements beyond Earth forces the invention of new governance structures adapted to isolation, resource scarcity, and existential dependence on technology.

Real-World Relevance

Informs Mars colonization planning, space law development, and governance models for isolated high-tech communities.
