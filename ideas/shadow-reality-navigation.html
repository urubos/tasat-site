Parallel Reality Navigation

Description

A protagonist travels through shadow realities (parallel dimensions that are variations of a central pattern) searching for allies and truth, where the ability to traverse realities is itself a form of power that others seek to control.

Real-World Relevance

Models strategic information-seeking across multiple conflicting sources, relevant to intelligence analysis, multiverse theory implications, and decision-making under deep uncertainty.
