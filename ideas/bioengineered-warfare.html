Bioengineered Military Technology

Description

Nations wage war using genetically engineered living weapons (airships grown from whale DNA, walking tanks) alongside traditional machinery, testing whether biological and mechanical military paradigms can coexist or one must dominate.

Real-World Relevance

Relevant to bioweapon conventions, synthetic biology military applications, and the paradigm tension between biological and electronic approaches to military capability.
