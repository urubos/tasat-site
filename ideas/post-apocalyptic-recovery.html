Post-Apocalyptic Recovery

Description

After civilizational collapse, survivors must rebuild institutions, supply chains, and social trust from scratch, testing which structures prove resilient.

Real-World Relevance

Relevant to disaster recovery planning, civilizational resilience, and debates about which institutions and technologies are most critical to preserve.
