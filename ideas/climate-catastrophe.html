Climate Catastrophe Scenario

Description

Environmental collapse forces societies into triage decisions about resource allocation, migration, and survival under planetary-scale stress.

Real-World Relevance

Directly applicable to climate adaptation planning, coastal retreat scenarios, and resource conflict projections.
