Humanity as Galactic Pawn

Description

Humanity achieves space travel only to discover the universe is already filled with alien civilizations who view humans as pawns in their own conflicts, subverting the assumption that space exploration means human agency.

Real-World Relevance

Models the scenario where entering a new domain (space, internet, AI) means becoming a minor player in an existing power structure rather than a pioneer.
